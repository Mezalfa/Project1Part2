Our Story

Vintage Vision is where machine learning meets timeless fashion. Our team of designers and engineers built a model that recognizes styles across decades — from 1920s flapper dresses to 1990s grunge jackets.

Upload an image, and our AI will analyze textures, patterns, and silhouettes to identify the era and even suggest where you can shop for similar pieces.
